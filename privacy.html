Privacy Policy

How Howard Resource Group protects your privacy and handles your data.

1. Overview

Howard Resource Group ("we," "us," "HRG") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at https://howardresourcegroup.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Contact Forms: When you submit a consultation request, we collect your name, email, phone, and message. This data is sent to Formspree for processing and is used to respond to your inquiry.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to track website visits, pages viewed, and user interactions. GA4 uses cookies and collects anonymized data like device type, location, and referral sourceâ€”but does not collect personally identifiable information.
  • Call/Text Tracking: When you click "Call" or "Text" buttons, we log these interactions in GA4 for conversion tracking, but no personal data is stored.
  • Cart Data: Product selections and cart contents are stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and are not sent to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide the services you request
  • To improve website performance and user experience
  • To track conversion metrics and optimize marketing efforts
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Formspree: Handles form submissions securely. Formspree Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics 4: Tracks website usage anonymously. Google Privacy Policy

Both services are GDPR-compliant and do not store personally identifiable information without your consent.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies for:

  • Analytics: GA4 cookies track user behavior (anonymized)
  • Theme Preferences: We store your theme choice locally in your browser
  • Cart: Cart contents are stored locally and not tracked via cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality, but the site will still be usable.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are in the EU (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to data portability

If you are in California (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal data is collected
  • Right to request deletion
  • Right to opt-out of data sales (we do not sell your data)

To exercise these rights, contact us at 678-421-4125 or email support@howardresourcegroup.com.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure form handling via Formspree (SSL/TLS)
  • No storage of sensitive payment data (we redirect to contact forms, not payment processing)
  • Regular security monitoring

However, no security system is 100% secure. If you suspect a security breach, contact us immediately.

8. Contact Information

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us:

  • Phone: 678-421-4125

  • Email: support@howardresourcegroup.com
  • Address: Atlanta, GA (see Google Business Profile for details)

9. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
